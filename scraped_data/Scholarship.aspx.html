MIITJEE MANTHAN SCHOLARSHIP SCHEME

ABOUT - It is a holistic platform for the students who are competing among thousands of aspirants across the nation in the field of IIT JEE and Medical.

MIITJEE- MANTHAN Mission- Our mission is to offer financial aid to the bright students with scholarship and help them to take their first step towards making their dream come true and become an engineer or doctor.

MIITJEE- MANTHAN Offers- It is one of the most respected and rigorous exam that identifies the most deserving candidates and awards them with scholarship up to 100 percent for our academic classroom coaching program.

MIITJEE- MANTHAN Eligibility- Students of class 7th, 8th, 9th, 10th, 11th and 12th are eligible for this exam.
